## 📖 Overview

**TWF Cattle CRM** is a high-performance, integrated ecosystem engineered specifically for the modern cattle industry. It bridges the gap between on-field procurement, farm operations, and front-end sales, providing a unified command center for stakeholders.

---

## 🏗️ Core Sub-Systems

### 1. 🔐 Control Management
> **The Governance Layer**
*Ensuring system integrity through rigorous access control and transparency.*

- **Super Admin Command Center:** Exclusive access to system-wide configurations and sensitive data.
- **RBAC (Role-Based Access Control):** Granular permission mapping for Managers, Riders, Accountants, and Procurement officers.
- **Immutable Audit Logs:** A chronological record of every action taken within the system, providing a "who, when, and what" for every data change.
- **Session Security:** Advanced monitoring of active user sessions and access points.

### 2. 📅 Bookings Management
> **The Revenue Engine**
*Transforming leads into loyal customers through streamlined sales workflows.*

- **Lead Lifecycle Tracking:** Capture and nurture potential customers from initial inquiry to final conversion.
- **Dynamic Order Management:** Real-time processing of cattle bookings with automated status updates.
- **Financial Transaction Ledger:** Secure recording of payments, deposits, and refunds.
- **Automated Invoicing:** Professional, brand-aligned PDF invoices generated instantly upon transaction completion.

### 3. ⚙️ Operations Management
> **The Logistics Hub**
*Coordinating the complex movement of goods and services with precision.*

- **Digital Challan System:** Paperless delivery notes with QR code verification for secure handovers.
- **Rider Dispatch & Tracking:** Real-time assignment and monitoring of delivery personnel.
- **Customer Support Portal:** Integrated ticketing system to resolve customer queries and maintain high satisfaction.
- **Process Bottleneck Analysis:** Visual tracking of operational stages to identify and eliminate delays.

### 4. 🚜 Farm Management
*Maximizing biological and operational efficiency on the ground.*

- **On-Farm Activity Logging:** Daily tracking of feeding schedules, vaccinations, and health checks.
- **Performance Analytics:** Monitoring weight gain, health trends, and overall animal performance.
- **Petty Cash Management:** Real-time tracking of on-farm cash handling and immediate operational expenses.
- **Inventory Control:** Management of farm supplies, feed, and medical stock.

### 5. 🛒 Procurement Management
> **The Sourcing Strategy**
*Optimizing the acquisition of livestock and managing field-level overheads.*

- **Animal Acquisition Workflow:** Standardized process for buying animals, including quality checks and source tracking.
- **On-Ground Expense Tracker:** Instant logging of travel, transport, and intermediary costs during procurement trips.
- **Strategic Sourcing Insights:** Data-driven analysis of procurement costs vs. animal quality.

### 6. 📊 Accounting & Finance
> **The Intelligence Suite**
*Comprehensive financial visibility for data-driven decision making.*

- **Consolidated Financial Dashboards:** Real-time P&L views pulling data from Bookings, Farm, and Procurement.
- **Expense Categorization:** Automated breakdown of operational vs. capital expenditures.
- **Cash Flow Forecasting:** Predictive models to assist in financial planning and investment.
- **Tax & Compliance Reporting:** Simplified reports for regulatory and tax requirements.

### 7. 📈 Performance Management
> **The Human Capital Optimizer**
*Driving team excellence through transparency and goal alignment.*

- **KPI Definition Engine:** Set specific, measurable targets for every team member.
- **Individual Performance Dashboards:** Real-time visualization of achievements against assigned targets.
- **Team Leaderboards:** Gamified performance tracking to foster healthy competition and motivation.
- **Appraisal Data:** Historical performance data to assist in annual reviews and promotions.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **System Requirements:** PHP 8.1+, MySQL 8.0+, Node.js 18+.
2. **Installation:** Run `composer install` and `npm install`.
3. **Environment:** Configure your `.env` with database and mail credentials.
4. **Launch:** Start the server and access the Super Admin dashboard to begin setup.
